KEYS PAN Research Abstract

This study investigates the relationship between vitamin D supplementation, omega-3 fatty acid intake, and exercise interventions on epigenetic aging clocks. Using data from the Precision Aging Network, we analyzed multiple biomarkers to assess the impact of lifestyle factors on biological aging trajectories.

Our findings suggest that combined interventions may have synergistic effects on slowing epigenetic aging, with implications for preventive health strategies and personalized medicine approaches.
